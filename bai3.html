One particular book which is recommended reading The Street Lawyer by John Grisham.
This book is about a lawyer who begins re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets, and realizes the harsh existence of the the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The STreet Lawyer is a great book. It is well written and intersting. Other books by John Grisham include The Firm, The Pelican Brief, and The Client
