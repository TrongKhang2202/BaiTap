IUH

The forerunner of the Industrial University of Ho Chỉ Minh University was Professional Tratmng School in Go Vap and founded December 1956. March 1999, the school was upgraded to College Industry IV and December 2004 School was upgraded to Ho Chỉ Minh University of Industry under Decision No 214/2004/QD - TTg ofthe Prime Minister.
